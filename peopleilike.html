This is a page I'm messing around with to test Bootstrap and customizing it. Right now there is only a navbar (still working on that dropdown function) with a link to a couple of web devs/writers/practicers of tom-foolery. I know of Mike Lacher and Chris Baker through the collaboration on Seinquest2000 - an immensely silly site/multimedia production that you must experience for yourself.

Mike Lacher

His work includes such marvelous projects asThe Film Thesis GeneratorandHate With Friends.

Chris Baker

A man who, amongst other things, developed this really cool site called Lightyear.fm, which tells you what would be playing on the radio the farther you traveled from earth. He also worked on an amazing Chrome extension called Unbaby.me, which served as a way to filter out unwanted Facebook baby photos from your Newsfeed. It has since gone open-source and renamed as Rather, which serves as a filtering extension for basically anything you don't want to see.
